## Welcome to the AcadSpace Admin Dashboard
This guide will help you navigate the new administrative tools.

### 1. How to Access the Dashboard
1.  Navigate to `[YourAppURL]/admin/login` (or click "Admin" from the main menu if logged in as an admin).
2.  Log in using your authorized Admin credentials. If you do not have access, contact your system administrator.

### 2. Managing Careers
The **Careers** tab allows you to maintain the database of recommended careers.
*   **To Add:** Click the "Add New Career" button in the top right. Fill out the form carefully, ensuring the RIASEC profile is formatted correctly (e.g., "RIA").
*   **To Edit:** Find the career in the table and click the "Edit" icon (pencil). Make your changes and click Save.
*   **To Delete:** Click the "Delete" icon (trash can). *Warning: Deletions are permanent and logged.*

### 3. Using the Analytics Pivot Table
The **Analytics** tab is your tool for uncovering trends in student data.
1.  **Select Fields:** Drag fields from the left panel into the "Rows", "Columns", or "Values" boxes.
2.  *Example:* To see which Grades are matching with which Career Buckets, drag `Grade` to Rows, and `Top Bucket` to Columns.
3.  **Exporting:** Click "Export to CSV" to download the current view for use in Excel or Google Sheets.

### 4. Viewing Audit Logs
The **Audit Logs** tab provides a read-only history of who changed what in the system. Use this to track down when a career was updated or deleted.
